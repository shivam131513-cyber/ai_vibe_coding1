## üìç Reporting a Hazard

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Navigate to Report Page**
   - Click **"Report Hazard"** in the navigation bar
   - Or click **"Report a Hazard"** button on homepage

2. **Fill in Hazard Details**
   - **Hazard Type**: Select from dropdown (Pothole, Broken Streetlight, etc.)
   - **Category**: Choose the most relevant category
   - **Severity**: Rate from Low to Critical
   - **Description**: Describe the hazard in detail

3. **Add Location** (Optional but Recommended)
   - Click **"Use Current Location"** to auto-fill
   - Or manually enter an address
   - The map will show a pin at the location

4. **Upload Photo** (Optional)
   - Click **"Choose File"** or drag and drop
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
   - Max size: 5MB

5. **Submit**
   - Click **"Submit Report"**
   - You'll receive a unique **Ticket ID**
   - Save this ID to track your report!

### Tips for Better Reports

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Be specific in descriptions
- Include exact locations
- Upload clear photos
- Rate severity accurately

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Submit duplicate reports
- Use offensive language
- Report non-urgent personal issues

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: How long does it take for reports to be addressed?**
A: It varies by severity and local authority response time. Critical issues are prioritized.

**Q: Can I edit a report after submitting?**
A: Currently, reports cannot be edited. Contact support if you need to update information.

**Q: What if my report is a duplicate?**
A: Duplicate reports are merged. You'll still get credit for reporting!

**Q: Can I report anonymously?**
A: No, you need an account to ensure accountability and prevent spam.

**Q: How do I delete a report?**
A: Contact support with your ticket ID to request deletion.

**Q: Why isn't my report showing on the map?**
A: Make sure you added location information when submitting.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works great on phones and tablets.
